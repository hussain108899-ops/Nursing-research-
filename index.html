About the Forum

Forum Overview

The Nursing Research & Innovation Forum (NRIF) is the flagship annual research and innovation event of the College of Nursing at Imam Abdulrahman Bin Faisal University.

The forum brings together students, faculty members, researchers, healthcare professionals, innovators, and strategic partners to showcase research excellence, foster collaboration, and accelerate innovation that improves healthcare outcomes and quality of life.

Aligned with Saudi Vision 2030, RDIA priorities, IAU's Research & Innovation Strategy, and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

  • Scientific excellence and evidence-based practice
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation
  • Strategic academic–clinical partnerships
  • Translation of research into societal impact
  • Development of the next generation of nursing researchers
